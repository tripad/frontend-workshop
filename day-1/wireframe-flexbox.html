Flexbox

Flexbox arranges items in one direction — either a row or a column.

What these properties do

display: flex Turns the element into a flex container. Its children become flex items.
justify-content Controls how items are spread along the main axis (left-right in a row).
align-items Controls alignment on the cross axis (top-bottom in a row). Try: flex-start, center, flex-end, stretch.
gap Sets space between items. Much cleaner than margin on individual items.
flex-direction Switches from row (default) to column. All the same rules apply, just rotated.
